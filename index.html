Personal Info

Mark John Lado is a Filipino technology and education enthusiast. He has established himself as an accomplished Information System Specialist in the education management industry, showcasing expertise in areas such as Object-Oriented Programming (OOP), Teacher Mentoring, Computer Hardware, Software System Analysis, and Web Development.

Having successfully completed his Master's degree in Information Technology at Northern Negros State College of Science and Technology, Sagay City, Philippines, Mark has further enriched his academic knowledge and expertise in the field. Throughout his career, Mark's passion for education has driven him to collaborate with various educational institutions in the Philippines, including Cebu Technological University, University of the Visayas - Danao Campus, Colegio de San Antonio de Padua, and Carmen Christian School Inc.

Mark has held roles as an ICT Instructor/Coordinator at Carmen Christian School Inc. and a Part-time Information Technology Instructor at the University of the Visayas. He has also served as a Faculty member at Colegio de San Antonio de Padua before joining the College of Technology and Engineering at Cebu Technological University, where he currently serves as a Faculty member.

Beyond his academic and professional achievements, Mark actively participates in the Philippine Society of Information Technology Educators, serving as a PSITE Member (IT). His research article titled "A Wireless Digital Public Address with Voice Alarm and Text-to-speech Feature for Different Campuses" has been published in Globus: An International Journal of Management & IT - A Refereed Research Journal.

Furthermore, Mark has demonstrated his expertise through the publication of four books on Amazon. These books, namely 'Mastering PC Troubleshooting and Operating Systems: A Comprehensive Guide', 'Embedded Systems: From Historical Development to Modern-Day Applications', 'Data Modeling and Process Analysis: A Comprehensive Guide - Volume I', and 'From Idea to Manuscript: A Step-by-Step Guide to Writing Your Nonfiction Book', exemplify his extensive knowledge in the field of technology and education.

Mark's unwavering commitment to education and his passion for technology have established him as a highly regarded Information System Specialist. Continuously pursuing excellence, he remains abreast of the latest advancements in the field, making him an invaluable asset to the academic community.
